About this app

  • Delay time: how long you have to hold down for the timer to start.
  • Inspection time: how long you have to inspect your puzzle.
  • By default best and worst are overall.
  • Options: make a new session; delete, clear, or export; and change the session name and description.
  • To make changes to an individual time, click/tap on that time.
  • Yes, this is a PWA! You can add it to your home screen or make a shortcut.
