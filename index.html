About this app

  • Delay time: how long you have to hold down for the timer to start.
  • Inspection time: how long you have to inspect your puzzle.
  • Best and worst are overall, not per session.
  • Create a new session by clicking the "New" button.
  • Options: delete, clear, or export, and change the session name and description.
  • To make changes to an individual time, click on that time.
